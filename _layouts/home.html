Content

> Talks


Talks will be about Free Software and Security. All the talks shall be given in English. The 2019 edition (last IRL edition) hosted 26 talks, grouped in 7 sessions.

> Workshops


2019 Pass the SALT hosted 9 workshops to discover new security free software projects. Network, theat intel and IAM. Come to discover and practice!

> Networking


Exchange experiences and create new relationships with attendees or speakers in a warm and open-minded ambiance.
